Name: Repeater Zombie
Difficulty: Medium/Hard
Connection: Uses the Repeater, on the Armory
Summon: RepeaterZombie
Melee: No
Distance: Hitscan
Type: Human, Undead
Brightmaps: No
Actor modification: No
ACS: No


A dangerous zombie with an interesting weapon. After a wind-up period, the Repater Zombie rapidly
fires a slightly-explosive hitscan attack. If it fires for long enough, the weapon overheats and
spins back down. Dangerous when allowed to keep firing, but very vulnerable until it does.
